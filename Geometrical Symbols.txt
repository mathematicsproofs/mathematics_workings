Here’s a comprehensive list of geometrical symbols, grouped by their meaning or usage:

---

### **Basic Geometric Shapes**
- Line: `↔` or `AB`
- Ray: `→` or `AB`
- Line segment: `¯` or `AB`
- Triangle: `△`
- Square: `□`
- Rectangle: `▭`
- Circle: `◯`
- Ellipse: `⋯`
- Polygon (general): `n-gon`

---

### **Angles and Measurements**
- Angle: `∠`
- Right angle: `∟`
- Acute angle: `< 90°`
- Obtuse angle: `> 90°`
- Degrees: `°`
- Radians: `rad`
- Minutes: `'`
- Seconds: `"`
- Arc: `⌒` or `∩`
- Central angle: `∠ABC`

---

### **Relationships Between Shapes**
- Parallel: `‖`
- Perpendicular: `⊥`
- Congruent: `≅`
- Similar: `~`
- Equivalent: `≡`
- Proportional to: `∝`
- Adjacent: No specific symbol (denoted by verbal description or notation)
- Complementary: `∠A + ∠B = 90°`
- Supplementary: `∠A + ∠B = 180°`

---

### **Properties of Circles**
- Circumference: `C = 2πr`
- Area: `A = πr²`
- Radius: `r`
- Diameter: `d`
- Chord: `¯AB`
- Tangent: `↔`
- Secant: `↔`
- Arc length: `s`

---

### **3D Geometry Symbols**
- Cube: `□³` or `3D box representation`
- Sphere: `◯³`
- Cylinder: `[ ]³` or `(solid with two circles and curved surface)`
- Cone: `(◯ ↓  apex at top)`
- Volume: `V`
- Surface area: `SA`

---

### **Coordinate Geometry**
- Point: `P(x, y)`
- Distance formula: `√((x₂ − x₁)² + (y₂ − y₁)²)`
- Midpoint: `M = ((x₁ + x₂)/2, (y₁ + y₂)/2)`
- Slope: `m = (y₂ − y₁) / (x₂ − x₁)`

---

### **Transformations**
- Reflection: `R`
- Rotation: `↻` or `↺`
- Translation: `(x + a, y + b)`
- Dilation: `(kx, ky)`
- Symmetry: `∼` (depending on context)

---

### **Common Constants and Ratios in Geometry**
- Pi: `π`
- Golden ratio: `φ ≈ 1.618`
- Euler’s number (used in spiral geometry): `e ≈ 2.718`

---

### **Common Measurements and Terms**
- Length: `l`
- Width: `w`
- Height: `h`
- Base: `b`
- Perimeter: `P`
- Area: `A`
- Volume: `V`
