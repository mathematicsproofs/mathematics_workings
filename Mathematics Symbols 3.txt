Here’s an expanded and comprehensive list of common mathematical symbols across various fields to ensure you have as complete a reference as possible:

---

### **Basic Operations**
- Addition: `+`
- Subtraction: `-`
- Multiplication: `×`, `·`, `*`
- Division: `÷`, `/`
- Equals: `=`
- Not equal to: `≠`
- Approximately equal to: `≈`
- Proportional to: `∝`
- Less than: `<`
- Greater than: `>`
- Less than or equal to: `≤`
- Greater than or equal to: `≥`

---

### **Arithmetic & Number Theory**
- Modulo: `mod`
- Divides: `|`
- Does not divide: `∤`
- Integer part (floor): `⌊x⌋`
- Fractional part (ceiling): `⌈x⌉`
- GCD: `gcd(a, b)`
- LCM: `lcm(a, b)`
- Summation: `∑`
- Product: `∏`
- Exponentiation: `xⁿ`
- Square root: `√`
- Cube root: `∛`
- nth root: `ⁿ√`
- Logarithm: `log(x)`
- Natural logarithm: `ln(x)`
- Absolute value: `|x|`
- Factorial: `n!`
- Percent: `%`

---

### **Set Theory**
- Set brackets: `{ }`
- Element of: `∈`
- Not an element of: `∉`
- Subset: `⊆`
- Proper subset: `⊂`
- Superset: `⊇`
- Proper superset: `⊃`
- Empty set: `∅`
- Union: `∪`
- Intersection: `∩`
- Complement: `A'` or `¯A`
- Difference: `A \ B`
- Cartesian product: `A × B`
- Power set: `𝒫(A)`

---

### **Logic**
- For all: `∀`
- There exists: `∃`
- Does not exist: `∄`
- Negation: `¬`
- Logical AND: `∧`
- Logical OR: `∨`
- Implies: `⇒`
- If and only if (iff): `⇔`
- Therefore: `∴`
- Because: `∵`
- Contradiction: `⊥`
- Tautology: `⊤`

---

### **Calculus & Analysis**
- Derivative: `d/dx`, `f'(x)`
- Partial derivative: `∂/∂x`
- Gradient: `∇f`
- Integral: `∫`
- Double integral: `∬`
- Triple integral: `∭`
- Line integral: `∮`
- Limit: `lim`
- Infinity: `∞`
- Differential element: `dx`
- Del operator (Nabla): `∇`

---

### **Geometry**
- Angle: `∠`
- Right angle: `90°`
- Parallel: `‖`
- Perpendicular: `⊥`
- Triangle: `△`
- Congruent: `≅`
- Similar: `~`
- Circle: `◯`
- Arc: `⌒`

---

### **Trigonometry**
- Sine: `sin`
- Cosine: `cos`
- Tangent: `tan`
- Cosecant: `csc`
- Secant: `sec`
- Cotangent: `cot`
- Inverse trigonometric functions: `sin⁻¹`, `cos⁻¹`, `tan⁻¹`
- Degrees: `°`
- Radians: `rad`

---

### **Probability & Statistics**
- Probability: `P(A)`
- Mean (average): `μ`
- Median: `M`
- Standard deviation: `σ`
- Variance: `σ²`
- Union (or): `∪`
- Intersection (and): `∩`
- Complement: `A'`
- Expected value: `E(X)`
- Covariance: `Cov(X, Y)`
- Correlation coefficient: `ρ`

---

### **Vectors & Linear Algebra**
- Vector: `v⃗`, `|v|`
- Dot product: `a · b`
- Cross product: `a × b`
- Magnitude: `||v||`
- Transpose: `Aᵀ`
- Determinant: `det(A)`
- Matrix: `[aᵢⱼ]`
- Eigenvalue: `λ`
- Eigenvector: `v⃗`

---

### **Advanced Topics**
- Kronecker delta: `δᵢⱼ`
- Dirac delta: `δ(x)`
- Tensor: `Tᵢⱼ`
- Fourier transform: `ℱ{f(x)}`
- Laplace transform: `ℒ{f(t)}`
- Partial differential equation: `∂²u/∂x²`

---

### **Greek Letters (Frequently Used in Math)**
- `α` (Alpha), `β` (Beta), `γ` (Gamma), `δ` (Delta), `ε` (Epsilon), `ζ` (Zeta), `η` (Eta), `θ` (Theta), `ι` (Iota), `κ` (Kappa), `λ` (Lambda), `μ` (Mu), `ν` (Nu), `ξ` (Xi), `π` (Pi), `ρ` (Rho), `σ` (Sigma), `τ` (Tau), `υ` (Upsilon), `φ` (Phi), `χ` (Chi), `ψ` (Psi), `ω` (Omega)
